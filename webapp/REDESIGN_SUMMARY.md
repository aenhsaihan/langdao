# LangDAO Design System Summary

## Typography
- **Headings**: Bricolage Grotesque (400-900 weight)
- **Numbers/Data**: JetBrains Mono (monospace for precision)
- **Body**: Lexend (300-700 weight)

## Colors
- **Background**: Dark gradient `from-[#0F0520] via-[#1A0B2E] to-[#0F0520]`
- **Primary Accent**: Amber to Orange gradient `from-amber-400 to-orange-500`
- **Secondary**: Cyan, Purple, Pink for variety
- **Text**: White with opacity variants (white/60, white/80)

## Component Patterns
- **Cards**: `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-sm border border-white/10 rounded-2xl`
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with hover scale effects
- **Inputs**: Dark with white/10 borders, rounded-xl
- **Labels**: Uppercase, tracked-out, text-xs, white/60

## Spacing
- Compact padding: p-4 to p-6 (not p-8)
- Moderate gaps: gap-4 to gap-6
- Rounded corners: rounded-xl to rounded-2xl (not 3xl)

## Font Sizes
- Page titles: text-4xl to text-5xl (not 7xl-8xl)
- Card titles: text-xl to text-2xl
- Numbers: text-3xl to text-5xl in JetBrains Mono
- Body: text-sm to text-base
- Labels: text-xs
